KiVA x Perception Test Challenge

KiVA evaluates a model’s capacity for abstract visual reasoning that even human children can achieve.
We are honored to present a guest challenge at the Perception Test Workshop, ICCV 2025 in Honolulu!

Visit Perception Test Site KiVA Webpage Challenge Dataset Terms & Conditions

KiVA Challenge is organized into 3 levels of difficulty.
